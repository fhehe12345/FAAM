### Questions:
1. Which project phase has the highest cost and why?  
2. How could you reduce costs without affecting quality?  
3. How does resource allocation influence your schedule and overall success?  

### Answers:
1. **Highest cost phase:** Dashboard Design & Implementation has significant costs because UI/UX design and visualization require skilled personnel.  
2. **Cost reduction:** Reuse templates or pre-built dashboard components instead of designing everything from scratch.  
3. **Resource allocation impact:** Assigning the right people to the right tasks improves efficiency and reduces unnecessary work, which supports project success.
