# ANAS AMSYAR BIN ANUAR

### Questions:
1. Which project phase has the highest cost and why?  
2. How could you reduce costs without affecting quality?  
3. How does resource allocation influence your schedule and overall success?  

### Answers:
1. **Highest cost phase:** Data Collection & Preparation is costly due to the need for acquiring datasets and preprocessing them accurately.  
2. **Cost reduction:** Automate parts of data cleaning and use smaller sample datasets for initial testing to save time and resources.  
3. **Resource allocation impact:** Properly distributing team members and equipment prevents bottlenecks and ensures smooth workflow.
